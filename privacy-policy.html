NEON Duel Legacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NEON Duel Legacy handles data.

Summary

  • NEON Duel Legacy does not require an account.
  • NEON Duel Legacy does not collect personal information.
  • NEON Duel Legacy does not send analytics or telemetry to the developer.
  • NEON Duel Legacy does not display third-party advertising.
  • NEON Duel Legacy stores a small settings file locally on your device so your preferences persist between sessions.

Local device data

The app stores a local settings file on your device. That file may include:

  • audio settings such as master, music, and sound-effect volume
  • control preferences such as light-wall input mode
  • visual settings such as trail brightness and glow intensity
  • display settings such as window mode, resolution, and VSync
  • a local debug toggle used for development builds

This data is stored locally on the device and is not transmitted to the developer.

Data not collected

Based on the current version of the app, NEON Duel Legacy does not collect or transmit:

  • name
  • email address
  • phone number
  • precise or approximate location
  • contacts
  • photos or videos
  • microphone or camera data
  • payment information
  • account credentials
  • advertising identifiers for developer analytics
  • gameplay telemetry sent to the developer

Sharing

NEON Duel Legacy does not share personal data with third parties because the current version of the app does not collect personal data from you.

Children

The current version of NEON Duel Legacy does not include chat, user accounts, user-generated content uploads, or direct social features.

Security

The current version stores only local settings on-device and does not operate a live backend for player accounts or player data.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app adds features such as analytics, advertising, online accounts, cloud saves, or multiplayer services. Any material change should be reflected here before the related version is released.

Contact

For privacy questions related to NEON Duel Legacy, use one of these public contact paths:

  • GitHub issues: https://github.com/mkoitz/retro-tron-game/issues
  • Developer contact details listed on the app's Google Play store listing
